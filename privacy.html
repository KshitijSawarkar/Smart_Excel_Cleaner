Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart Data Cleaner. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our data cleaning tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

2. Data Processing and Storage

Local Processing Only: All data processing happens locally in your browser. Your files, data, and information never leave your device or get transmitted to our servers.

No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your uploaded files, data, or personal information.

Temporary Processing: Data is held temporarily in your browser's memory only during the cleaning process and is cleared when you close the browser or navigate away from the page.

3. Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number
  • We do not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not store your uploaded files or processed data
  • We do not share your data with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • CDN Libraries: We use Tailwind CSS, Font Awesome, SheetJS (xlsx), and Papa Parse from CDNs for styling and functionality
  • Google Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns (anonymous data only)
  • Google AdSense: We may display advertisements through Google AdSense to support our free service

5. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally in your browser, your data remains secure on your device. We implement security best practices for our website infrastructure, but your actual data files never leave your computer.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
